About me

Brief history

Currently I work at NHS digital as a graduate UX designer.
Prior to that, I worked as a TV news producer reporting on european current affairs for TV Asahi, a Japanese national broadcaster.
I started taking illustration commisions from the start of 2020, but I've been drawing all my life.
I am from Japan, but have also lived in England, Denmark and Hongkong.



Education

I have a MSc from the University of Oxford in Visual, material and museum anthropology. As well as a BA from the University of Tokyo in Japan.


Skills

Ethnographic research methods, including observation study and interviewing.
As I am a visual thinker, I draw illustrations and graphs to help process and analyse things.
I speak English, Japanese and Swedish.
I also write sometimes.



Tools

Adobe Illustrator, photoshop, indesign, XD, figma, sketch, html, css.
Hand drawn prototyping, tablet illustrations, and visual thinking.
