About me

Brief history

Right now, I work at NHS digital as a User centred design graduate.
Before that, I worked for 2 years as a TV news producer reporting on european current affairs for TV Asahi, a Japanese national broadcaster.
And prior to that, I was studying anthropology in various places including Oxford, Tokyo, Copenhagen and Berlin.
I started taking illustration commisions from the start of 2020, but have been drawing all my life.


Skills

As I am a visual thinker, I draw illustrations and graphs to help process, analyse and communicate things.
My background is in anthropology and journalism, so some of my favourite qualitative research methods include ethnography and interviewing.
I speak English and Japanese, and I'm learning Swedish.
And finally I also illustrate and write about some little thoughts I have.
