About me

Brief history

Right now, I work at NHS digital as an User centred design graduate.
Before that, I worked for 2 years as a TV news producer reporting on world affairs for TV Asahi, a Japanese national broadcaster.
And prior to that, I was studying anthropology in various places including Oxford, Tokyo, Copenhagen and Berlin.

In my spare time I do commisioned illustrations.


Education

Skills

As I am a visual thinker, I draw illustrations and graphs to help process, analyse and communicate things. I enjoy facilitating workshops, telling and listening to stories and making digital, hand-drawn and knitted prototypes.
My background in academia and investigative journalism means I highly respect thorough research and analysis, and those two things are vital in my design process.

I speak English and Japanese, and am currently learning Swedish.
