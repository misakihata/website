About me

I am an user centred designer at NHS digital, where I’ve worked on the NHS service manual, the NHS test and trace service and making NHS health campaigns more accessible. I am currently leading on implementing inclusive design and research practises organisation wide.

I’m also part of On the Mend design studio, creating bespoke events, workshops and artworks to educate people on the societal impacts of health and well-being.

Skills

I am a visual thinker, I draw illustrations and graphs to help process, analyse and communicate things. I love facilitating workshops, telling and listening to stories and making digital, hand-drawn and knitted prototypes.
My background in anthropology and journalism means I highly respect thorough research and analysis, and those two things are vital in my design process.

I speak English and Japanese, and am currently learning Swedish.
